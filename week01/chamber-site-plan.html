Logan Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Logan Chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

The Logan Chamber of Commerce serves as a hub for local business networking, community development, and economic growth. It provides resources and support to businesses and entrepreneurs in Logan.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming networking events organized by the chamber?
  • How can local businesses become members of the chamber?
  • What initiatives is the chamber taking to promote economic development in Logan?

Color Schema

Main color: #007BFF (used for headings and links)
Accent color: #FFC107 (used for buttons and highlights)

Typography

Main font: Roboto (used for body text)
Heading font: Oswald (used for headings)
